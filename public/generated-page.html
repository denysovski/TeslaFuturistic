Frequently Asked Questions

What is the range of Model S Plaid?
Model S Plaid has an estimated range of 396 miles (EPA est.) on a single charge. Range may vary based on driving conditions, wheel selection, and other factors.
How fast is Supercharging?
With V3 Supercharging, you can add up to 200 miles of range in just 15 minutes. The peak charging rate is 250kW.
Does it come with Autopilot?
Yes, every new Tesla comes with Basic Autopilot included. This enables your car to steer, accelerate and brake automatically within its lane. Full Self-Driving capability is available as an upgrade.
