Frequently Asked Questions

Model S Plaid is rated up to 396 miles (EPA estimate). In daily use, range depends on speed, weather, elevation, and wheel setup. The onboard energy graph and live route planner continuously adjust predictions and suggest charging stops, helping drivers plan trips with high confidence.
With V3 Supercharging up to 250 kW, Model S can recover substantial range quickly. The navigation system preconditions the battery before arrival to optimize charge speed, and route guidance dynamically adapts based on traffic and charger availability.
Every new Tesla includes core Autopilot capabilities for lane-centered highway driving assistance. Additional driver-assistance packages can be added, while regular over-the-air updates refine usability, safety logic, and system responsiveness over time.
Many owners report lower operating costs thanks to reduced routine maintenance and lower fueling cost per mile versus premium gasoline sedans. Exact savings depend on electricity pricing, annual mileage, local incentives, and financing terms.
